How to Join dotpool.io (#299)

Joining our pool is quick and easy. Follow these simple steps to stake your DOTs with dotpool.io:

  1. Visit the Polkadot Staking Dashboard.
  2. Log in with your Polkadot wallet and navigate to the "All Pools" section.
  3. Search for our pool by its name dotpool.io or its ID: #299.
  4. Select our pool and follow the prompts to stake your DOTs securely.

Once completed, your staking rewards will start accruing, and you’ll enjoy 0% commission, forever!

How We Earn

At dotpool.io, our earnings come from operating our own Polkadot validator. While we charge 0% commission for our validator as well, this may increase to a maximum of 3% in the future to cover operating costs.

The commission rate is carefully managed to ensure fairness and sustainability for all users. Unless the price ratio of running a node compared to Polkadot validator rewards significantly shifts, this cap will remain.
